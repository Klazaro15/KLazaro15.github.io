My beloved grandfather who passed away

Here's a time line of my Grandfather:

  • 1948 - Born in Bicol
  • 1959 - He started working with his parents in a farm.
  • 1965 - He worked at a soap factory in Manila.
  • 1971 - He came to Baguio and learned Tailoring
  • 1973 - He married Fe Balangue, whom he met at a tailoring shop.
  • 1974 - They started having a baby daughter and named her Luningning Lazaro.
  • 2022 - Dies at the age of 74

"There is no one in the world like your granma and grandfather. They are your blood, your lineage, and sometimes they are the person who you hold nearest and dearest. Grandparents add so much color and vibrance to life, and theywon't be around forever. Make sure that you tell them how loved and appreciated they are while they are still with you here on earth."
